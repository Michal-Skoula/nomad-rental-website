About the property

This rental unit for digital nomads near Forest Kamýk is located in Prague and offers accommodation with a balcony and free Wi-Fi. Interesting places to visit include: Vyšehrad, the National Museum and Charles Bridge, St. Vitus Cathedral and Prague Castle, all less than 12km away from this accomodation.

The Astronomical Clock is 12 km from the accomodation, Old Town Square is 12 km away. Václav Havel airport is 27 km away from where you'll be staying.
